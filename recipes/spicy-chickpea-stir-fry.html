Spicy Chickpea Stir-fry

Description

A protein-rich, spicy, and satisfying dish that's ready in under 15 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onion and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
  4. Cook until tomatoes soften, then add chickpeas.
  5. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes. Garnish with coriander.
