Banana Oat Pancakes

Description

Healthy, fluffy pancakes made with just 3 main ingredientsâ€”no refined sugar or flour.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • A pinch of salt and cinnamon (optional)
  • Oil or butter for cooking

Steps:

  1. Blend banana, eggs, and oats into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a pan and grease lightly with oil or butter.
  3. Pour small portions of batter and cook 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Serve warm with honey, fruits, or peanut butter.
