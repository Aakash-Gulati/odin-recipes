Creamy Garlic Mushroom Toast

Description

A savory and comforting open-faced toast with creamy garlic mushrooms, perfect for a quick breakfast or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 2 tbsp cream or milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread slices until gliden.
  2. In a pan, heat butter and saut√© garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add mushrooms, cook for 5-6 minutes until soft.
  4. Add cream, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Spoon the mixture over toast and garnish with parsley.
